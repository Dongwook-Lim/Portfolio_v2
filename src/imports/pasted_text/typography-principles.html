NICOLAS

POUSSIN

(01) Nicolas Poussin was the leading painter of the classical French Baroque style, although he spent most of his working life in Rome.
(02) He returned to Paris for a brief period to serve as First Painter to the King under Louis XIII and Cardinal Richelieu, but soon returned to Rome and resumed his more traditional themes.
(03) His work is characterized by clarity, logic, and order, and favors line over color. Until the 20th century he remained a major inspiration for such classically-oriented artists.
